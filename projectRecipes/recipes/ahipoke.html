Ahi Poke

Poke, pronounced as po-kay (or po-kee for mainstream culture) is a Hawaiian Favorite. This seafood combination is noted as a stable in the Locals' diet. It is extremely versatile and the recipes are made to taste. The main ingredients consist of fresh seafood, maui onion, and soy sauce. Everything can be substituted to whatever your heart desires. Be sure to eat this with a fresh and warm bed of rice!

Ingredients

  • ahi tuna (sashimi grade)
  • maui onions - you can use sweet onions or shallots
  • green onions
  • soy sauce
  • seasame oil
  • chili garlic sauce
  • seasame seeds

Steps

  1. Pat the ahi tuna dry, then neatly cut into small 1/2 inch cubes. Then place into a bowl.
  2. Cut and then add the shallots, green onions, and pour the soy sauce, seasame oil, chili garlic sauce, and seasame seeds.
  3. Gently toss together. Serve immediately or cover and refrigerate.
